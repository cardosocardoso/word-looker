Word Looker

Words and phrases you add here are detected inside PDFs when the overlay is on. The list syncs across your signed-in Chrome browsers and is stored only in your browser — nothing leaves your machine except Chrome's own sync.

Drag rows to change priority — top row shows first in the overlay panel. Matching is always case-insensitive. Enable whole word for short terms or acronyms (e.g. AHP) so they don't match inside unrelated words. Heading boost makes matches inside bold / large headings surface first when iterating.
